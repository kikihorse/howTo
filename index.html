Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • 3/4 cup butter

  • 1/2 cup white sugar

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

  2. Use mixer to cream butter, sugar, vanilla and eggs
  3. Add the dry ingredients and mix

Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

These chocolate cookies, coated in icing sugar are fudgy in the center but are crisp on the outside.

Yields:50 cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup natural unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp enstant espresso or coffee granules
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Mix cocoa powder, sugar and vegetable oil with electric mixer or hand mixer
  2. Mix eggs in one at a time then add vanilla
  3. Whisk flour, baking powder, salt and espresso in a seperate bowl
  4. Mix dry and wet ingredients together
  5. Cover the dough and let chill for 4 hours or overnight
  6. Preheat oven to 350째F and line baking sheets with parchment paper. Place powdered sugar in a wide bowl
  7. Roll clumps of dough into small balls and roll in the powdered sugar. Place on baking sheet.
  8. Bake for about 10 minutes

Gingersnap Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup molasses
  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp ground ginger
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350째F
  2. Cream butter and sugar, then add egg and molasses and mix well
  3. Mix in flour, baking soda, salt, ground ginger and ground cinnamon.
  4. Cover and refrigerate dough for 15-20 minutes
  5. Scoop into balls and place on baking sheet
  6. Bake for 10 minutes
